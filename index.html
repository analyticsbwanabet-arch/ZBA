About the Awards

Celebrating Zambia's
Betting Industry Excellence

The Zambia Betting Awards is the premier recognition platform for excellence in the nation's betting and gaming sector. Since 2023, we have celebrated outstanding achievements, innovation, and commitment to customer satisfaction across the industry.

Our Mission

To establish and maintain the definitive standard of excellence in Zambia's betting industry, recognizing companies that demonstrate exceptional performance, innovation, and dedication to providing superior experiences for customers while upholding the highest ethical standards.
